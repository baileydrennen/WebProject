My kid is 9 years old, which team would they be on?
Depending on their date of birth, they would be apart of the half pint team.

When do practices start and where can we find the schedule?
We generally start pitchers and catchers for the live pitch Pints team in March. Practices will start in May. The schedule can be found at the State Line League website. It will also be posted on this website.
